# User Guide

## Getting Started

1. Sign up for an account at [our website](https://example.com).
2. Log in to your account.
3. Click on "New Project" to start a new animation project.

## Uploading Images

1. In your project, click "Upload Image".
2. Select an image file from your computer.
3. Wait for the image to upload and process.

## Creating Animations

1. Select an uploaded image.
2. Choose an animation type from the sidebar.
3. Adjust animation parameters as desired.
4. Click "Preview" to see the animation.
5. Click "Save" to add the animation to your project.

## Exporting Projects

1. Open your project.
2. Click "Export" in the top right corner.
3. Choose your desired export format.
4. Wait for the export to complete and download your animated image.

For more detailed instructions or troubleshooting, please contact our support team.
